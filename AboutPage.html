About Pixelloid

Our Mission
To be the Metaverse’ most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they might need in the virtual world, and endeavors to offer its customers the lowest possible prices.
How we work
Pixelloid’s Metaverse Limited is an unlisted Metaverse company. It is run remotely by passionate curators, devoted to providing customers with a great range of virtual goods, while still diligently managing limited virtual stock.
Our vision and values
Our corporate values appear in everyday working life and guide our strategic direction and corporate planning.
  • We place the customer experience at the core of all we do
  • We are committed to our people
  • We operate our business ethically
  • We give back to our communities
  • We grow our business profitably
  • We strive continually to improve
Our history
In the Metaverse in 2030 there was a NFT salesman named Tim Zroc. Tim received an order from the Pixelloid brothers’ virtual outlet on the virtual planet, Voxel and was impressed by their store - the menu was simple and cheap but the pixels were good; the catchphrases were made in-store; and the entities were more advanced than usual.

Tim made them an offer. “Let me create a Pixelloid virtual store, accessible on Web 2.0 and I’ll give you half of one per cent of the gross sales for the use of the name and the idea.”

The Pixelloid brothers accepted and Tim opened his virtual store hosted on a small web server. He began to build the business by growing its product range. By 2040, his website had grown a thriving customer-base, and Tim continues to this day to improve Pixelloid into the best virtual goods store it can be.
